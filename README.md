**COFFEE EXPANSION ANALYSIS — INSIGHTS & RECOMMENDATIONS**

---

**1. Estimated Coffee Consumer Base by City**

Assuming 25% of each city's population are potential coffee consumers:

- Dhaka leads by a massive margin at 25.74 lakh estimated consumers, followed by Chattogram (8.08 lakh) and Gazipur (6.69 lakh).
- The remaining 11 cities each fall below 2.5 lakh, with Brahmanbaria at the bottom (0.64 lakh).
- Dhaka's consumer base is more than 3x the next largest market, making it the single biggest opportunity in the country.

---

| City | Total Revenue (BDT) | Q4 2023 Revenue (BDT) | Customers |
|---|---|---|---|

| Rangpur | 9,44,120 | 3,02,500 | 42 |
| Dhaka | 8,60,110 | 2,70,780 | 39 |

| Gazipur | 7,50,420 | 2,38,490 | 68 |

- Rajshahi is the #1 revenue city despite having a far smaller population than Dhaka, a clear sign of high customer spending power.
- Dhaka ranks 3rd in total revenue with only 39 unique customers, meaning each customer spends heavily. The customer base is just too small relative to its population.
- Q4 2023 was strongest in Rajshahi, likely driven by seasonal gifting or end-of-year promotions.

| Rajshahi | 9,000 | 24,197 | 173 | Excellent ROI |
| Gazipur | 10,000 | 11,036 | 147 | Strong ROI |
| Mymensingh | 10,800 | 11,644 | 157 | Strong ROI |
| Rangpur | 10,500 | 22,479 | 250 | Very Good |
| Dhaka | 16,700 | 22,054 | 428 | Good (high rent) |
| Chattogram | 15,500 | 8,704 | 574 | Risky |
| Sylhet | 12,500 | 6,124 | 446 | Poor ROI |
| Khulna | 11,500 | 5,987 | 500 | Poor ROI |

- Rajshahi has the lowest avg_rent_per_customer (BDT 173) and the highest avg_sale_per_customer, unbeatable efficiency.
- Chattogram is the biggest red flag: high estimated_rent (BDT 15,500) but one of the weakest avg sale/customer figures (BDT 8,704). The numbers don't justify investment without significant marketing spend.
- Dhaka is expensive but still viable, its high avg sale per customer offsets the high rent, but growing the customer base there is critical.

---

**6. Strategic Recommendations**

**Tier 1 — Prioritize Now**
- **Rajshahi**: Best overall, highest revenue, highest avg sale per customer, lowest rent burden. Double down here.
- **Rangpur**: Strong revenue and avg sale per customer with moderate rent. Ideal second city.
- **Dhaka**: Only 39 customers tapping a 25.74 lakh consumer base. The biggest untapped opportunity in the dataset, a focused acquisition push here could transform the business.

**Tier 2 — Watch & Grow**
- **Mymensingh & Gazipur**: High customer counts with decent total revenue. Good for high-volume, lower-ticket retail formats.
- **Chattogram**: Large potential market but poor ROI at current sales levels. Needs deeper market penetration before further investment.

**Tier 3 — Low Priority**
- **Sylhet, Khulna, Narayanganj, Comilla**: Poor avg sale per customer relative to rent. Not recommended for near-term expansion.
- **Brahmanbaria, Savar**: Smallest populations and lowest revenue, hold off.

**Product Strategy**
- Lead with the **Cold Brew Coffee Pack** in all campaigns, it drives the most revenue by far.
- Bundle **Coffee Beans (500g)** with Cold Brew to increase average basket size.
- Expand branded merchandise (Tote Bags, mugs), demand signals a real lifestyle brand opportunity.
